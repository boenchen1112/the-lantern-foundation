BrightNotes

Turning Music into Meaningful Action

BrightNotes is a student-led music initiative that transforms artistic talent into social impact. Students plan and host fundraising concerts, organize hospital and community performances, and use music as a bridge between youth and underserved communities.

Funds raised are donated to partner organizations supporting stateless children and other vulnerable groups, ensuring that creative expression translates into tangible support. Currently, BrightNotes works closely with the CS Music Club from Chingshin Academy, regularly performing at hospitals to bring comfort and companionship through music.

Beyond fundraising, BrightNotes empowers students to speak about social issues publicly — through performances, storytelling, and digital advocacy — allowing music to become a voice for children who are often unheard.

LightAid

Healthcare Beyond Legal Boundaries

LightAid focuses on supporting children excluded from Taiwan's national health insurance system — particularly those without legal nationality. Without a national ID, a single illness can become financially devastating for families already living without legal protection.

Student teams work alongside corporate sponsors and professional partners to secure stable medical funding, ensuring consistent access to treatment, checkups, and essential care. More than financial assistance, LightAid provides students with firsthand experience in resource coordination, accountability, and long-term impact management.

Transparent monthly reporting ensures that every contribution is traceable and measurable — building trust and sustainability into the system from the ground up.

HeartLink

Building Relationships That Last

HeartLink coordinates a volunteer system focused on long-term companionship. Students are recruited, trained, and guided before visiting — ensuring thoughtful, respectful, and sustained engagement with the children they serve.

In addition to community visits, HeartLink organizes public engagement and fundraising initiatives, sharing real stories and inviting broader society into conversation.

We believe change is built through continuity, not one-time gestures. Companionship, when sustained, becomes its own kind of light.

"A steady presence is the most powerful tool we have for healing."
— Alvin Chen, Co-Founder
