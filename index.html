Our Mission

Empowering students.
Restoring dignity.

The Lantern Foundation is a student-founded nonprofit that empowers youth to engage in meaningful, structured action — while directly supporting children who have been left without nationality, healthcare, or stable education.

Our story began with a message: "50 undocumented immigrant babies hidden in the mountains like refugees." What we discovered was impossible to ignore.

Read Our Story

Our Commitments

To restore dignity, stability, and belonging to children without nationality.

To empower students to become the light that sustains that change.

Lasting impact grows when more lights are willing to shine — not from one lantern alone.

Why We Exist

Every child deserves to be seen.

Stateless children in Taiwan grow up without legal identity, healthcare, or stable education. We refuse to let them grow up unseen.

How It Began

A message. A discovery.
A decision to act.

"50 undocumented immigrant babies hidden in the mountains like refugees."

What started as a three-person group chat became consistent visits, a student concert, a structured volunteer system, and finally — a foundation built not only to support children, but to build a platform where young people can transform concern into commitment.
